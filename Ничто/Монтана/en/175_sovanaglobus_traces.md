## No timestamp | Meter vs Cubit

When was the decision made to measure further with "meter", how did those who measured with "cubit" react?

The metric system was introduced in France in 1795. Became mandatory in 1840 — after 45 years. Almost half a century of resistance.

**Merchants — sabotaged.** Old measures allowed manipulation (seller's cubit vs buyer's cubit). A single standard killed this margin.

**Peasants — rebelled.** In 1812 Napoleon temporarily brought back old measures due to popular resistance.

**Aristocracy — against.** Cubit = local power. Each feudal lord had his own "cubit". Meter = centralization, loss of control.

**Scientists — for.** Lavoisier, Condorcet. They were later executed (not for the meter, but telling).
